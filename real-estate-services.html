Our Real Estate Services

We offer comprehensive real estate services under our brand The Swiss Agency Immobilien, tailored specifically for expats and international clients looking to invest in Swiss property.

Property Sales

Expert guidance through the Swiss property selling process. We handle valuations, marketing, viewings, and negotiations to maximize your property's value.

Buyer Representation

Dedicated buyer's agent service to find your ideal property, negotiate the best terms, and guide you through Swiss property acquisition regulations.

On & Off Market Search

Access exclusive off-market properties and comprehensive market search to find properties that match your exact criteria before they go public.

Mortgage & Financing

Navigate Swiss mortgage requirements with our financing support. We connect you with banks and help optimize your financing structure.

Notary & Legal Support

Full notary support including power of attorney arrangements for international clients who cannot be present for signings.

Property Rental Services

Find quality tenants for your investment property. We handle marketing, tenant screening, and lease management.

Holiday Homes

Purchase vacation properties in Switzerland's tourist zones. We specialize in helping foreigners acquire holiday homes in permitted areas.

Off-Market Properties

Exclusive access to properties not listed publicly. Our network provides opportunities for discreet transactions and unique investments.
