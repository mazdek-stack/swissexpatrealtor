Expert Immigration Support

Navigate Swiss Immigration with Confidence

Switzerland's immigration framework is highly regulated, quota-based, and administered at both federal and cantonal levels. Our Immigration Services provide structured, compliant, and reliable support for employers and private individuals navigating Swiss work and residence authorisations.

We advise, prepare, and manage immigration procedures with precision, ensuring full alignment with Swiss law while minimising delays and operational risk. We support both corporate clients and private individuals, acting as a trusted intermediary between applicants, employers, and Swiss authorities.

Our Immigration Expertise

Work & Residence Permits

EU / EFTA and Non-EU / EFTA nationals

Permit Application Services

  • Assessment of eligibility under Swiss immigration law
  • Strategic advice on permit type and cantonal procedures
  • Preparation and submission of complete permit applications
  • Coordination with cantonal migration and labour market authorities
  • Monitoring of timelines, quotas, and approvals

Permit Categories

  • L Permit: Short-term residence permits
  • B Permit: Residence permits
  • G Permit: Cross-border commuter permits
  • C Permit: Settlement permits (advisory support)

Non-EU / EFTA Immigration

Highly regulated / approval-based procedures

  • Eligibility and labour market assessment
  • Quota availability analysis at cantonal and federal level
  • Employer support for justification and compliance requirements
  • End-to-end application management
  • Close follow-up with authorities throughout approval process

Employer-Sponsored Immigration

  • Review of employment contracts for immigration compliance
  • Coordination with employers, HR, and legal stakeholders
  • Guidance on salary thresholds, role justification, and seniority requirements
  • Support with work authorisation prior to employment start

Family Reunification

  • Advisory on eligibility and documentation requirements
  • Application preparation for spouses and dependent children
  • Coordination with cantonal migration offices
  • Timeline management and compliance guidance

Registration & Post-Arrival Formalities

  • Communal registration coordination
  • Permit issuance follow-up
  • Biometric appointment support
  • Permit renewals and amendments

Permit Renewals & Status Changes

  • Residence and work permit renewals
  • Change of employer or canton
  • Permit upgrades (where applicable)
  • Ongoing compliance monitoring
