Corporate Relocation Excellence

In today's global economy, attracting and retaining international talent is crucial for business success. Swiss Expat Realtor specializes in corporate relocation services that remove the complexity and stress from employee mobility, ensuring your valuable team members arrive ready to perform.

Our boutique approach means we treat each employee relocation with the same care and attention we would give to our own family members. This personalized service, combined with our systematic processes and local expertise, delivers consistent, high-quality outcomes that reflect positively on your organization.

  • Tailored neighborhood and school previews for every family profile.
  • Temporary housing, orientation, and culture briefings scheduled early.
02 Housing

Housing & Accommodation

Executive-ready housing searches with transparent data, remote decision tools, and meticulous handovers.

  • Curated property shortlists graded by budget, commute, and lifestyle.

Administrative Support

We remove paperwork friction by centralizing all regulatory, financial, and compliance tasks.

Family Integration

Ongoing Support
