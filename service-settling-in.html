Relocating to Switzerland should feel effortless. Our Settling-In & Arrival Support is designed for executives, senior professionals, and internationally mobile families who value precision, discretion, and absolute reliability.

Our Services at a Glance

Everything you need to establish your new life in Switzerland, handled with Swiss precision.

Resident Registration & Residence Permit

Deadline: Within 14 days of arrival / before first working day

  • Registration with the local commune or city authority
  • Residence permit application (where applicable)
  • Preparation and review of required documentation
  • Appointment scheduling and, if required, attendance
  • Full compliance with cantonal regulations

Swiss Driving Licence Exchange

Deadline: Within 12 months of arrival

  • Eligibility assessment for licence exchange
  • Application preparation and submission
  • Coordination with the cantonal Road Traffic Office
  • Timely exchange to avoid legal or insurance risks

Bank Account Opening

  • Guidance on suitable Swiss banking partners
  • Preparation of required documentation
  • Support throughout the account opening process
  • Ensuring readiness for salary payments, rent, and daily expenses

Health Insurance Setup & Guidance

Deadline: Within 3 months of taking up residence

  • Explanation of the Swiss healthcare system
  • Advice on suitable health insurance providers
  • Coordination of policy setup and registration
  • Ensuring coverage from the date of arrival and avoiding penalties

Utility & Connectivity Setup

Recommended: Aligned with move-in date

  • Organisation of internet, TV, and phone services
  • Contract setup and coordination of installations
  • Ensuring immediate connectivity upon move-in

Public Transport Passes & Registration

  • Advice on local, regional, and national transport passes
  • Support with registration and subscriptions
  • Assessment of commuter, student, or corporate options
