Our Story

Swiss Expat Realtor was born from personal experience. As former expatriates ourselves, we intimately understand the challenges of relocating to Switzerland— the language barriers, the competitive housing market, the complex administrative processes, and the emotional journey of starting fresh in a new country.

Founded in 2022, we set out to create the relocation service we wished had existed when we first arrived. A service that combines Swiss efficiency with international empathy, professional expertise with personal care, and local knowledge with global perspective. Today, we've helped over 70 families and professionals successfully relocate to Zurich, Zug, Schwyz and Basel.

Each success story reinforces our commitment to providing boutique, personalized service that transforms the complexity of international relocation into a supported, stress-free journey. What sets us apart is our genuine understanding of both sides of the relocation experience.

We've built lasting relationships with landlords, property managers, and local service providers throughout Switzerland, allowing us to open doors that would otherwise remain closed to newcomers. Our multilingual team speaks the language of both expat needs and Swiss expectations, bridging cultural gaps seamlessly.

Our Mission

To be the trusted partner for international professionals and families relocating to Switzerland, providing comprehensive, personalized support that ensures a smooth transition and successful integration into Swiss life.

We believe that successful relocation goes beyond finding a place to live—it's about creating a foundation for long-term happiness and success in Switzerland. Every service we offer, every connection we make, and every decision we guide is focused on this singular goal.
